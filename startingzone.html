The Starting Zone

Disclaimer

Always consult a doctor before starting any kind of fitness program. If you are an absolute beginner, I recommend working with a fitness professional to ensure you are doing all the movements properly. To determine if you are ready for a fitness program fill out a PAR-Q form (Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire).

Welcome! This program is called the starting zone because I think about where characters in online role playing games start their journey. They encounter basic enemies to understand the mechanics of the game and become incrementally stronger. Starting off in fitness is the same idea. Once you know the basics and start to gain better control and awareness of your body, you’ll become stronger and eventually the challenges you faced in the starting zone won’t seem very hard at all. So thank you for letting me be a part of your first month into your fitness journey!

How the Challenge Works

This is a four week challenge where everyday you will alternate between an excercise day and a recovery day. Every day you'll have a choice of what you will do. The exercise day will have choices between set workouts, fitness games, videos, or audio workouts for either strength or cardio. The recovery days will include choices of low intensity exercise, yoga, or stretch sequences. All the exercises in this challenge are ten to twenty minutes. The point of this challenge is not to lose a certain amount or gain a certain amount of muscle, but to explore a variety of ways to be active and to kickstart your exercise habits so hopefully you will continue after these four weeks and discover a healthy lifestyle that you will maintain and enjoy.
