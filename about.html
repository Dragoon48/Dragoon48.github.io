About me

Trainer Ross


My name is Ross and I'm an ACE certified personal trainer. I got my start in fitness with karate in high school. From there I got into running, triathlon, weightlifting, yoga, and enything new! I've learned that fitness motivation can be difficult, but once you get started it's hard to stop. That's why I chose to focus on the social aspects of physical activity with group hikes, a weekly run club, and fitness classes. Being active should be fun and not a chore. If you'd like to schedule a time to meet me, please visit the contact page. I hope to meet you soon!
